Security Policy

Supported Scope

This policy applies to:

  • https://henry07.vercel.app
  • https://github.com/1501henify
  • https://github.com/ifyOke0

Reporting a Vulnerability

Report responsibly via:

  • Email: henify.1501@gmail.com
  • GitHub: github.com/1501henify
  • GitHub: github.com/ifyOke0

Please include description, reproduction steps, impact, and proof-of-concept if available.

Response Timeline

  • Initial response: within 7 days
  • Status update: within 14 days
  • Fix or mitigation: as soon as possible

Safe Harbor

Good-faith researchers will not face legal action if they:

  • Avoid data destruction or service disruption
  • Do not access other users’ data
  • Avoid social engineering or physical attacks

Recognition

Valid disclosures may be acknowledged publicly. No monetary rewards are offered.
